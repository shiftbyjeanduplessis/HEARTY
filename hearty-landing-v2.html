Why most GLP-1 users struggle

The medication works.
The gaps between doses are where it gets lost.

01

Not enough protein → muscle loss

Reduced appetite sounds like a win — but eating too little protein causes your body to break down muscle alongside fat. You lose weight, but the wrong kind.

02

Too little water → nausea & fatigue

Dehydration is one of the most common GLP-1 side effects — and most people don't realise they're under-drinking until nausea and headaches set in.

03

No resistance training → skinny-fat outcome

Without strength work, rapid weight loss often costs muscle mass — leaving people lighter but weaker, with worse long-term health outcomes.

04

Bad days → you fall off track

Nausea, exhaustion, or just feeling awful are part of GLP-1 life. Without a plan for those days, most people skip everything — then feel guilty and spiral. That's what Support Mode is for.

✦

Hearty addresses all four — with side effect logging that helps you and your doctor respond faster, plus protein guidance, hydration targets, and workouts that adapt to how you feel each day.

Common questions

Everything you need to know

Is Hearty medical advice?
No. Hearty is a habit and tracking tool, not a medical service. Always follow your prescriber's guidance on medication, dosing and health decisions.
Does it replace my doctor or dietitian?
Absolutely not. Hearty gives you the daily structure your clinical team doesn't have time to provide between appointments.
Is my health data private?
Yes. Your data is encrypted, stored securely, and never sold to third parties. You own your data and can export or delete it at any time.
What is Support Mode?
Support Mode is a one-tap setting for the days when nausea, exhaustion or just feeling rough makes your normal routine impossible. Hearty softens movement targets, swaps meals for easy options, replaces pressure with reassurance — and still keeps your streak going. Bad days are part of the journey. Hearty plans for them.
What is the 30-day money-back guarantee?
If Hearty isn't right for you within the first 30 days, email us and we'll refund in full. No questions, no hoops, no fuss.
Does it work on iPhone and Android?
Yes, Hearty works on both iOS and Android. You can also access it from any web browser on desktop or tablet.
What happens after 18 months?
Your 18 months covers your full treatment journey. After that, you'll have the option to extend at a low renewal price — we'll never pressure you.
Can my clinic use this too?
Yes — we offer a B2B patient dashboard for clinics with real-time visibility across your full GLP-1 panel. Pricing is quoted per clinic size.
