Community

Private accountability circle.

Community is optional. When it is on, Hearty can turn safe app actions into simple encouragement updates. Weight numbers, photos, medication details, and side-effect details are never shared here.

Off
Community is off No Community feed events will be created or shown. Turn it on when you want private accountability updates.
Default is offCommunity starts disabled. Events are ignored until the user turns it on.
Safe events onlyAllowed events: workout complete, weigh-in logged, meal plan generated, support mode activated, and lesson complete.
No private dataNo exact weight, photos, medication details, side-effect details, or open text posts are shared in v1.
Local v1 storageThis version stores Community events in localStorage. Supabase circle sharing can be added later when the product rules are final.
