Questions

The honest answers.

Is Hearty medical advice?
No — and we'll never pretend it is. Hearty is a daily companion for structure, tracking and consistency. It works alongside your prescribing doctor, not instead of them. If something feels wrong, speak to your doctor.
What if I have a terrible week and don't use it?
That's exactly why Support Mode exists. Even on your worst days, a quick log and a one-tap switch keeps you connected to your routine without the guilt. Your streak stays alive. You come back when you're ready.
I'm not very fit. Will the workouts work for me?
Yes. Hearty starts from wherever you are — including chair-supported exercises for people who are just beginning or have limited mobility. Every programme progresses at a pace your body can actually handle.
Does Hearty work for all GLP-1 medications?
Yes. Whether you're on Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Saxenda or Rybelsus — the principles are the same. Hearty is built around the biology of GLP-1, not a specific brand.
Is my health data private?
Hearty is built privacy-first: no ads, no selling data, and the social layer is optional. You can use it as a private companion if you prefer.
What happens after my year is up?
You'll have the option to renew at the same rate. Nothing auto-renews without your permission. Your data stays with you regardless.
